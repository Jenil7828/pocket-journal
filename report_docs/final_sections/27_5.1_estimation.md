# 5.1 Estimation

## Effort Estimates (Person-Hours)
- Data preparation and annotation: 80–120 hours (mood-labelled entries, summarization pairs)
- Model fine-tuning (RoBERTa, BART): 120–160 hours including experiments and hyperparameter search
- Inference integration and optimization: 60–100 hours
- Recommendation pipeline tuning and evaluation: 80–120 hours
- Insights prompt engineering and validation: 40–80 hours
- Documentation and report preparation: 40–60 hours

## Resource Estimates
- Development environment: 1–2 developer machines (CPU)
- Training and benchmarking: GPU instance(s) with ≥16 GB VRAM (access as required)
- Storage: 100 GB for model artifacts, datasets and cache

## Cost Estimates (Indicative)
- Cloud compute for training and evaluation: variable; budget for hourly GPU pricing depending on provider.

These estimates assume a two-person team and iterative cycles of development and evaluation.
